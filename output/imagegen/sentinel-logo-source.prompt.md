# Sentinel logo source

`sentinel-logo-source.png` is the original logo concept for Sentinel's macOS menu-bar
companion: a monochrome shield and abstract S wrapped around a watchtower/beacon.

Generation constraints:

- Original enterprise-software identity with no copied product assets or trademarks.
- Bold, simple silhouette that remains recognizable at 16–22 px.
- Monochrome geometry that can become a macOS template image.
- No text, gradients, shadows, or small disconnected details.

The native-integration phase will derive the final true-alpha macOS template assets and
validate their optical weight at 1x and 2x menu-bar sizes. This source image is the approved
brand concept, not the final platform-sized bitmap.
